Itachi Uchiha

Description

Itachi was a prodigious member of the Uchiha clan, known for his wisdom and loyalty. Despite being branded a traitor after massacring his clan, Itachi’s actions were revealed to be a selfless act to protect the village. A master of genjutsu, he’s one of the most beloved and tragic characters in Naruto.

Powers and Abilities

  • Sharingan and Mangekyō Sharingan
  • Amaterasu
  • Tsukuyomi
  • Susano’o (with the Yata Mirror and Totsuka Blade)
  • Genjutsu Mastery

Quote

"People live their lives bound by what they accept as correct and true. That’s how they define reality. But what does it mean to be ‘correct’ or ‘true’? They’re merely vague concepts… their reality may all be a mirage."
