Naruto Uzumaki

Description

Naruto is a spirited ninja of Konohagakure who dreams of becoming Hokage. Known for his unyielding determination and his love of ramen, Naruto is the Jinchūriki of Kurama, the Nine-Tails. His journey to be acknowledged and protect his friends defines his growth.

Powers and Abilities

  • Shadow Clone Jutsu
  • Rasengan and Rasenshuriken
  • Sage Mode
  • Kurama Chakra Mode
  • Six Paths Sage Mode

Quote

"When you give up, your dreams and everything else, they’re gone."
